Turning data into a lighthouse

Data is like a lighthouse atop a cliff, illuminating the darkness for boats looking for a landmark. As a guide, it provides reliable markers in the uncertain waters of decision-making. Yet, more than that, it acts as a powerful spotlight, revealing the hidden contours of the landscape to guide us toward clearer horizons. Data, like a persistent light, brightens the path of understanding, allowing us to navigate confidently through challenges and discover perspectives we might not have seen otherwise.

Hello, I'm Alexandre

From my early experiences in environmental consultancy to roles in project management and entrepreneurship, I consistently assumed the role of the go-to person for creating and optimizing Excel tools. As a natural problem-solver, I actively sought opportunities to streamline processes, automating tasks to enhance efficiency. My commitment to simplifying colleagues' workflows allowed them to focus on their core responsibilities, delegating time-consuming tasks to computer tools.

My journey took a significant pivot during my tenure at Phenix, where I immersed myself in the realm of data analytics using Looker to analyze client data. It was there that I truly recognized its potential as a transformative tool. In my role of Customer Success Manager, I not only analyzed and interpreted data but also implemented it strategically to guide clients in minimizing food waste.

This pragmatic experience fueled my fascination with data optimization, prompting me to pursue a Data Analytics bootcamp intensively covering a spectrum of tools from Excel and BigQuery to Python, ELT methodology and DBT, machine learning and visualization tools like Looker and Power BI.

Now, as a data analyst, my passion lies in leveraging data-driven insights to unravel patterns, automate processes, and empower decision-making. The journey from process optimization to data analytics embodies my commitment to transforming challenges into opportunities, making data a powerful ally in addressing complex issues and contributing to a more informed and sustainable future.
